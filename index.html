About this map

"Food desert" is the official term, but many Indigenous advocates prefer food apartheid — because lack of access is a policy-driven injustice, not a natural feature. Orange zones show census tracts where low-income residents live more than 1 mile from a supermarket. Darker zones indicate severe gaps. Data: USDA Food Access Research Atlas + Maricopa County GIS.
